What is PhantomNet?

PhantomNet is an AI-driven Security Operations Center (SOC) platform designed to automate and enhance your cybersecurity posture. Our event-driven security model ensures threats are identified and mitigated instantly, providing autonomous response capabilities that reduce human workload and minimize breach impact.
